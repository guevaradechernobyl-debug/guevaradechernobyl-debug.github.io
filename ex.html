Character Profile

DIO OVER HEAVEN

The god-like incarnation of DIO, achieved after obtaining the Saint's Corpse and rewriting reality itself. Having transcended the limits of human potential through sheer will and ambition, DIO Over Heaven wields a power that surpasses all stands and all timelines. He does not merely defeat his enemies — he rewrites them out of existence. In Eyes of Heaven, he stands as the final boss and the most powerful being across all of JoJo's history.

HEAVEN ASCENSION

The World Over Heaven grants DIO the ability to overwrite reality with his fists. Anything struck by his Stand is rewritten — living beings, objects, even the laws of the universe itself. Time stop is merely a bonus. His true power is the absolute rewriting of all existence, making him effectively omnipotent within any timeline he enters.
