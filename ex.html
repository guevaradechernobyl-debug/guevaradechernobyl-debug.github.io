Abilities

REALITY OVERWRITE

The World Over Heaven can rewrite anything — matter, memory, time, and fate itself. A single touch rewrites the very nature of its target at a fundamental level.

TIME STOP

The original power of The World — stopping time for an infinite duration. In his ascended form, DIO's time stop transcends all limits.

STAND ERASURE

Can overwrite and erase other Stands entirely, stripping them of their power and identity. No Stand can persist against DIO's absolute authority.

DIMENSIONAL CONTROL

Grants dominion over space and dimensional boundaries. DIO may reach across realities, pulling beings from their timelines into his own domain.
