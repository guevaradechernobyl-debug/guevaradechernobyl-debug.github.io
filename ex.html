THE WORLD OVER HEAVEN
Stand · User: DIO · 天国へ到達する世界

Having obtained the Saint's Corpse and rewritten the very laws of reality, DIO ascended beyond all limits. He is not merely a villain — he is a god who remade the universe in his image. His Stand, The World Over Heaven, transcends time, space, and existence itself. In Eyes of Heaven, he stands as the supreme final antagonist: a being whose will alone can erase and overwrite all of creation.

Stand Statistics
HEAVEN ASCENSION

The World Over Heaven grants its user the power to overwrite reality itself. Any surface struck by its fists is rewritten — people, objects, timelines, even the fabric of the universe. Time stop is merely secondary to his true dominion: the absolute rewriting of all that exists. No Stand, no power, no god can oppose what has already been overwritten.
