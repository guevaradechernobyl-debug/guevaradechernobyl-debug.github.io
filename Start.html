Why This Blog Exists

It started as a school project.

A requirement. Something to finish and forget about. That was the plan.

But there's something about broken code. Error messages that made no sense, Small fixes that somehow made everything work. The quiet satisfaction of something finally clicking into place.

It's a specific kind of feeling, and once you notice it, it's hard to ignore.

So what began as a requirement became something I kept returning to. And at some point the question stopped being "why keep going" and started being "why not just put it out there?"

So here it is. Public. A little unfinished. Exactly as it should be.
