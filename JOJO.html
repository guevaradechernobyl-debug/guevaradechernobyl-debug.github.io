The Explanations & Reasonings

Death Note being in GOD TIER absolutely deserves its place. The story flow is insanely tight, the mind games are on another level, and every plot twist feels intentional instead of forced. Light vs L is still one of the most iconic rivalries in anime history, and the atmosphere alone carries the show into legendary status.

Berserk also earns its GOD TIER spot without question. The themes, the brutality, the tragedy — it's dark in a way that actually means something. It's not edgy for the sake of being edgy; it's raw, uncomfortable, and unforgettable.

Code Geass… this show is a masterpiece, especially the ending. The build-up, the sacrifices, the absolute mind-blowing conclusion — I was genuinely freaking out watching it. Lelouch's character arc is one of the best-written in anime, period.

Now JoJo's Parts 3 and 4 (Might be a little biased) easily the best of what I've watched so far. The Stand battles, the creativity, the characters, the sheer personality of these parts — it's peak JoJo.

TOP TIER shows are insanely good and just barely miss GOD TIER. Incredible world-building, strong casts, and moments that genuinely stick with you.

Made Me Cry speaks for itself. These anime didn't just entertain me — they emotionally destroyed me in the best way possible.

HIGH TIER is stacked with really strong anime. Great pacing, solid storytelling, and characters that are easy to get invested in.

MID TIER doesn't mean bad — not even close. Enjoyable, good moments, just not unforgettable.

LOW TIER is where cracks start to show. Pacing issues, weak writing, or wasted potential.

SHIT TIER… yeah. These annoyed me, bored me, or felt like a waste of time. No hate if you like them — just not for me.

Haven't Yet Watched — still on my radar. Who knows, some could end up GOD TIER once Araki gets them animated. (I'd probably be married with kids by the time it gets animated, but oh well.)
